Our Story

What started as an idea, an experiment in the small kitchen in the 11th floor of the apartment building has now transformed into Game Day Grill. It all began with two friends, Rami and Lorent that expanded their experimental takeout business into a proud, full fledged restaurant. A space where sports fans, and fresh, delicious food unites.

Rami and Lorent have been friends since the 7th grade, and they have gone from software developer roomates to the new and proud owners of Game Day Grill. The pair consistently work on new creations- hoping you will enjoy them!
